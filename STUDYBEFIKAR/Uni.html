Tips from the Nest

Only include the highest degree that you've earned or are in the process of earning.
What if I'm not enrolled full time? Certain lenders on the Sparrow platform will lend to students who are enrolled less than full time.
Why can't I find my school? Double check the spelling of your school. For example, if you searched for “UNC” try “University of North Carolina” instead.
Does graduation date affect eligibility? Lenders are typically willing to provide loans to Freshman, Sophomores, Juniors, Seniors, Graduates, PhDs, and pre-professionals.
